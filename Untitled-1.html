About Us

Maa Kamla Chandrika Jee Teacher's Training College came into existence in 2007. It is situated in jehanabad district of Bihar. The College is affiliated to Magadh University Bodhgaya. College admits students to its graduate level program- Bachelor of Education (B.Ed), Which is two year full time programme. The programme is offered as per the approval of National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), Bhubaneswar.In essence education leads to knowledge in turn knowledge leads to achievements and achievements leads to fulfilment , Each letter of the year EDUCATION enshrines an ideal, a value These are respectively Enlightenment, Dedication, Understanding, Character, Action, Through, Integrity, Originality and Nationhood.

Our Courses

  • D.El.Ed - Diploma in Elementary Education.
